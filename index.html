01 — About the Game

What Waits
in the Heat

Dead Sands is a survival horror game set across the desolate basin and range country of Southern New Mexico — a land of brutal sun, ancient silence, and things that should not exist.

You are stranded. Your vehicle is gone. The nearest town is a two-day walk across open desert — if the desert were safe. It is not.

Manage heat, thirst, and exposure while investigating the disappearances that have plagued this stretch of land for decades. The danger is real. The landscape is your enemy as much as anything else.

Built by a solo developer, learning in public. Every location, mechanic, and horror is a work in progress — and this site is where you can follow along.
